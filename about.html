About Meshtastic + MeshCore

CNYmesh is actively exploring both Meshtastic and MeshCore as complementary ways to build resilient, community-owned communications across Central New York.

Why Meshtastic is strong

  • Mature ecosystem: broad hardware support and established tooling
  • Large community: lots of documentation, examples, and peer support
  • Practical deployments: proven for local messaging and field operations

Why MeshCore is promising

  • New ideas: fresh architecture and protocol experimentation
  • Growing momentum: active exploration in community mesh projects
  • Future optionality: helps avoid single-protocol dependency

Why we are exploring both

  • Resilience: more than one path keeps the network adaptable
  • Learning: side-by-side testing improves local deployment decisions
  • Inclusion: members can participate with whichever protocol fits their goals
  • Regional growth: dual exploration helps us interconnect with more communities

Central New York Focus

We are focusing on Onondaga, Oswego, Madison, Oneida, Cayuga, and Cortland counties while coordinating with Rochester, Buffalo, Ithaca, and Albany.

The fastest way to follow current Meshtastic and MeshCore efforts is through our Discord and Facebook groups.
