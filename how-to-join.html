How to Join CNYmesh

It’s simple and affordable to get started. Whether you’re new to radios or a seasoned ham, this guide will have you online quickly.

Step 1 — Get a device

Popular choices: T-Beam, Heltec V3, or LILYGO T-Echo. Most cost $30–$100.

Step 2 — Install the Meshtastic app

Install on Android/iOS, pair over Bluetooth, set your handle/callsign, and configure basic settings.

Step 2A — Join us on social media (optional/recommended)

Connect with the CNYmesh community for support, updates, and collaboration:

  • Facebook Group: Join our Facebook community
  • Discord Server: Join our Discord for real-time chat
  • Email updates: hello@cnymesh.org

Step 3 — Join the CNYmesh channels

CNYmesh uses multiple Meshtastic channels to organize communication. The Primary channel is for node discovery and general chat, while Secondary channels serve specific roles like regional mesh chats and local alerts.

CNYmesh Channel Structure

Channel Name Channel Role Purpose
LongFast Primary Node discovery, chatting with nodes outside the CNYmesh network
CNYmesh Secondary Primary internal mesh communication for Central New York
CNYmesh-EAS Secondary Local weather alerts and other emergency notices for Central New York

How to Join CNYmesh Channels

  1. Open the Meshtastic app on your device
  2. Scan the QR Code below
  3. Select Replace when prompted
  4. Click Accept to confirm the channel configuration

CNYmesh Channel Configuration QR Code
Scan with your Meshtastic app to automatically join our channels

Step 4 — Configure LoRa Radio Settings

For optimal performance on the CNYmesh network, configure your device with these specific LoRa settings:

LoRa Configuration

  • Use Modem Preset: True
  • Modem Preset: Long_Fast
  • Frequency Offset: 0.0
  • Region: United States
  • Hop Limit: 3 (minimum) - 7 (maximum)
  • TX Enabled: True
  • TX Power: 30
  • Frequency Slot: 20
  • Override Duty Cycle: True
  • Override Frequency: 906.875
  • Ignore MQTT: False
  • OK to MQTT: True

Step 5 — Configure MQTT Settings

Enable MQTT to connect your node to the wider mesh network and allow remote monitoring:

MQTT Configuration

  • MQTT Server: Default Meshtastic MQTT server
  • Root Topic: msh/US/NY/CNY

This connects your node to the Central New York mesh topic for regional coordination.

Step 6 — Host a node (recommended)

  • Place near a window or outdoors, weather-protected
  • Elevation matters: rooftops, towers, or hills
  • Use USB or solar for 24/7 uptime
