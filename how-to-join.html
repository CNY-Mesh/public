How to Join CNYmesh

It’s simple and affordable to get started. Whether you’re new to radios or a seasoned ham, this guide will have you online quickly.

Step 1 — Get a device

Popular choices: T-Beam, Heltec V3, or LILYGO T-Echo. Most cost $30–$100.

Step 2 — Install the Meshtastic app

Install on Android/iOS, pair over Bluetooth, set your handle/callsign, and configure basic settings.

Step 3 — Join the CNYmesh channel

Use the app to join our shared community channel so local nodes can link up and relay messages.

Step 4 — Host a node (recommended)

  • Place near a window or outdoors, weather-protected
  • Elevation matters: rooftops, towers, or hills
  • Use USB or solar for 24/7 uptime
