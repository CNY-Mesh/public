Other Mesh Networks in New York

CNYmesh is part of a growing ecosystem of community mesh networks across New York State. These groups are working to create interconnected coverage throughout the region.

New York City & Metro

New York Mesh

NYC's community mesh network focusing on internet connectivity and emergency communications across the five boroughs.

Western New York

BuffaLoRa and SnyderMesh

Meshtastic network covering the Buffalo and Western New York region, building LoRa-based mesh connectivity.

Capital Region

Upstate Mesh

Mesh network serving Albany, Schenectady, Troy, and surrounding Capital Region communities.

Hudson Valley

Hudson Valley Mesh

Community mesh network covering the Hudson Valley region from Westchester to Columbia County.

Catskills Region

Sullivan County Mesh (KMesh)

Serving Sullivan County and the Southern Catskills Mountains with Meshtastic coverage.

Southern Tier

Southern Tier Mesh

Mesh network development across New York's Southern Tier region.
